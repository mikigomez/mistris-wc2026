📋 How it works

  • Create your profile — pick a name and a 4-digit PIN. Your PIN is yours only. Nobody else can edit your predictions.

  • Predict each match — before it kicks off, pick the score and your goalscorers. Once the match starts, predictions are locked forever.

  • Watch the points roll in — results update automatically. The leaderboard updates in real time. No admin needed.

  • Win the tournament — whoever has the most points after the final on 19 July wins.

📏 Prediction rules
1️⃣

One score per match. You pick one exact scoreline, which also determines your winner/draw pick.

⚽

Max 2 goalscorers per team, 4 total per match. Any position — striker, midfielder, defender or keeper.

🔒

Predictions lock at kickoff. The moment a match starts, your picks are frozen. No edits allowed.

👁️

Other people's picks are hidden until the match kicks off. You can't copy anyone before placing your prediction.

Tip: Picking Messi (striker, 5 pts) is safe but everyone will pick him. Picking a midfielder like Bellingham (10 pts) or a defending header specialist (15 pts) could be the move that wins you the whole thing.

⚠️ Fair play & anti-cheat

Predictions are locked at kickoff. The site checks the match start time and blocks any prediction once the match has begun. There is no way around this.

Other players' picks are hidden before kickoff. You cannot see what your friends predicted until the match starts — so copying is impossible.

Your profile is PIN-protected. Nobody can log in as you or change your predictions without your 4-digit PIN. Keep it to yourself.

Name your profile immediately. Once a name is taken, nobody else can use it. If someone takes your name before you, contact the group admin to reset it.

📅 Tournament format

48 teams, 12 groups of 4. Each team plays 3 group matches. The top 2 from each group plus the 8 best third-placed teams (32 total) advance to the Round of 32. From there it's straight knockout: Round of 16, Quarter-finals, Semi-finals, and the Final on 19 July at MetLife Stadium, New Jersey.
